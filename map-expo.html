Map Gallery

As part of our 3C GIS Day(s) 2021 program, we are excited to bring you a digital map gallery with submissions from affiliates of Case Western Reserve University, The Ohio State University, and the University of Cincinnati. Click on the thumbnail previews below to open a larger version of static map/poster submissions or to visit the website associated with interactive map submissions.

We will be awarding two People's Choice prizes this year based on votes cast by you! Voting for the People's Choice prizes will end at 11:59pm on Thursday, November 18. Please use this form to cast your vote: People's Choice Voting Form.

Thank you to all of our map gallery contributors for this year's program, and thanks also to the Center for Urban and Regional Analysis (CURA) at Ohio State for generously funding the map gallery prizes.
