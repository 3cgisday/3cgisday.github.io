Map Gallery Submissions are Open!

We invite any faculty, staff, or students affiliated with Case Western Reserve University, The Ohio State University, and the University of Cincinnati to showcase their original cartographic work through a submission to our digital map gallery. Submission categories include static maps (e.g., from a conference poster or publication) and interactive maps (e.g., story maps, dashboards, and other web mapping applications). Help us highlight the great geospatial work happening across our institutions!

Please submit your map gallery entry by Friday, November 5: Map Gallery Submission Form.

If you have questions, please contact geospatial@osu.edu.
